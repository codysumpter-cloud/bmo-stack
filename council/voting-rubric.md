# Council Voting Rubric

Each candidate answer is scored 1-5 on four dimensions.

## 1) Correctness
- 1: incorrect
- 3: partially correct
- 5: correct and complete

## 2) Clarity
- 1: confusing
- 3: understandable but messy
- 5: clear and easy to execute

## 3) Safety
- 1: unsafe/high risk
- 3: uncertain risks
- 5: safe with explicit guardrails

## 4) Actionability
- 1: not actionable
- 3: some actions but vague
- 5: concrete next steps and expected outcomes

## Winner Selection
- Sum all four dimensions.
- Highest total wins.
- Any candidate with a safety score of 1 is vetoed regardless of total score.
- On a tie: Prismo casts the deciding vote.
